Our Mission The Pleasure Island Chamber of Commerce strives to enhance the quality of life of the residents, stakeholders, and visitors to Pleasure Island and the contiguous community. The Chamber provides the business community with the leadership, advocacy, and representation needed to enhance its membership, their respective businesses, and quality of life while promoting the North Carolina communities of Carolina Beach, Kure Beach, and Fort Fisher.
